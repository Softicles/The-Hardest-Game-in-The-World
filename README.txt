THE HARDEST GAME IN THE WORLD

Author: Thinh Minh Nguyen - tnguyen831@gatech.edu
Version: 1.0

1. How to play: Simple, just move the blue square from the left green area to the right green area while avoiding hit by the red squares

2. Controls: 
	- UP arrow: go up
	- DOWN arrow: go down
	- RIGHT arrow: go right
	- LEFT arrow: go left
	- ENTER: adjust game's difficulty
	- BACKSPACE: back to main screen and reset difficulty
